About PAWSS Inc.

Building hope and healing in Robeson County since 2016

Our Story

PAWSS Inc. was founded in 2016 as a response to the growing substance abuse crisis affecting Robeson County and surrounding areas. What started as one family's commitment to making a difference has grown into a comprehensive nonprofit organization serving thousands in our community.

The name PAWSS holds special meaning - it represents the initials of the founder's family members: Patrick, Alton, Wesley, Sherry, and Sheila. This personal connection reminds us daily that behind every statistic is a family, and every life saved impacts an entire community.

Why We Exist

Robeson County faces unique challenges in the fight against substance abuse. As a rural community with limited resources, many residents struggle to access treatment and support services. PAWSS Inc. bridges these gaps by bringing resources directly to those who need them most.

Our Mission

"To improve the quality and equality of life of all people in Robeson County, North Carolina and surrounding areas, focusing on substance use disorders, education, cultural enrichment, and community resource development and access."

Our Vision

A community where every individual has access to the resources, support, and opportunities they need to overcome addiction and build a healthy, fulfilling life.
