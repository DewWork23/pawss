Volunteer Opportunities

Recovery Support Specialist

Provide peer support and mentorship to individuals in recovery.

  • Personal recovery experience preferred
  • Compassionate and non-judgmental
  • Available 4-8 hours per week
Regular Commitment

Event Volunteer

Help with community events, fundraisers, and awareness campaigns.

  • Flexible schedule
  • Good communication skills
  • Team player
Flexible

Administrative Support

Assist with office tasks, data entry, and program coordination.

  • Basic computer skills
  • Attention to detail
  • Reliable and organized
Part-time

Transportation Driver

Help individuals get to treatment, meetings, and appointments.

  • Valid driver's license
  • Clean driving record
  • Reliable vehicle
Flexible

Community Educator

Present prevention programs in schools and community centers.

  • Public speaking skills
  • Knowledge of substance abuse issues
  • Background check required
Project-based

Naloxone Box Monitor

Check and restock community naloxone boxes regularly.

  • Reliable transportation
  • Brief training provided
  • 1-2 hours per month
Monthly
