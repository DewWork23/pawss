Our Programs

Comprehensive support for prevention, treatment, and recovery

Stop The Pain Initiative

Flagship Program

The Stop The Pain Initiative is our comprehensive response to the opioid crisis affecting Robeson County. Since 2016, this program has been at the forefront of prevention, intervention, and recovery support services.

Community Naloxone Distribution

Free naloxone (Narcan) boxes strategically placed throughout the community for immediate access during overdose emergencies.

  • 24/7 accessible locations
  • Training on proper administration
  • Regular restocking and maintenance

Recovery Ambassador Program

Peer support specialists who have lived experience with recovery provide mentorship and guidance.

  • One-on-one peer support
  • Recovery navigation assistance
  • Community advocacy

Let's Talk Recovery

Weekly support groups and educational sessions creating safe spaces for open dialogue about recovery.

  • Weekly group meetings
  • Educational workshops
  • Family support sessions

Recovery Care Coordination

Comprehensive case management connecting individuals to treatment, housing, and support services.

  • Treatment placement assistance
  • Benefits navigation
  • Housing support
